Tut-Lab Week 8

Aims:

  • Understand the organization of an agile team

  • Understand the nature of agile planning and estimation

  • Start planning for the team-based project

Preparation:

  • Review the material on agile software processes

Agile Software Processes

  • Under guidance from your tutor/demonstrator, begin to develop an agile plan for the team-based project

  • Your objectives should be to:

    • Develop an initial shared understanding of the project

    • Decide who will take on the various Scrum roles in the project

    • Agree on regular times for daily standups and sprint reviews

    • Begin to consider some of the epics, user stories and tasks that constitute the project

    • Think about the resources required to undertake the project (including any gaps in your knowledge)
